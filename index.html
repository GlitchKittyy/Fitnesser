FAQ


Q: What is fitnesser's purpose?

A: Fitnesser was made to make simple fitness tasks even simpler, Like making a week training schedule, all to save you time.



Q: Why doesn't my made schedules save?

A: Fitnesser is Client-based, That means that generated results is only at your device and so it does not save. After you generated your schedule you can choose to download your schedule as a image.
