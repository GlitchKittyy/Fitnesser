# Fitnesser (v3.0)
A workout app to make your simple workout tasks even simpler
## Functions
In v3.0 (latest):
  -  Workout planner
      -  Makes a week schedule based on RNG
  -  Unit converter
      -  Easily transfer KG to LBS and reverse
  -  Workout selector
      -  Randomly chooses a muscle group for you

## How did it start?
I (GlitchKittyy) started developing Fitnesser for my own purpose to make my fitness tasks easier, but then friends wanted to use it too for their fitness benifits, and now we are here.
